Martí Gimeno Ortí


I was born in Terrassa in 1997. I've always been interested in computer graphics and science.


In 2009 I discovered Blender and it has been my main tool to create 3D artworks and animations since then. I enjoyed to learn Blender watching tutorials as well as trying it by myself. 7 years after, I was hired by tenured lecturer Jose Luís Valero, I gave a 3 hours introductory course on 3D software Blender to university employees.

In 2013 I passed the Youth and Science program application. This program promotes scientific careers among teenagers. Thanks to this program, I spent one month in Institute of Photonic Sciences in 2014, and another month in Weizmann Insitute of Science in 2015.

Now I'm currently studying Physics degree in Autonomous University of Barcelona
